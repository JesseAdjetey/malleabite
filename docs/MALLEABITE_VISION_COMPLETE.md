### **2. AI-Powered Scheduling (Mally AI)**
**Purpose**: Remove friction from calendar management through natural language

**What Makes it Special:**
- **Natural Language**: "Schedule team meeting tomorrow at 2 PM" ‚Üí Done

- **Real-time Sync**: Changes appear instantly across devices
- **Event Types**: Regular events, todos-as-events, recurring events
- **Visual Organization**: Color coding, icons for alarms/reminders/todos
- **Drag & Drop**: Convert todos to calendar events by dragging

### **4. Cross-Module Intelligence**
**Purpose**: Tools that work together, not in isolation

**Examples:**
- Todo marked "Urgent & Important" in Eisenhower ‚Üí Suggested for today's schedule
- Calendar event ‚Üí Automatically starts Pomodoro timer when it begins
- Meeting invite accepted ‚Üí Adds to calendar + sets reminder
- Task completed ‚Üí Updates both todo list AND calendar event
